Lab Instructions

This page contains links to the individual lab instructions for Math 217.

NOTE: All these links take you to HTML versions of the labs. But if you change the link extension to “.pdf”, you will get a more print-friendly version, with spacing for student answers to each question.

  • Lab 1 introduces you to the RStudio environment and shows you how to carry out basic descriptive statistics.
  • Lab 2 shows you how to create graphs and carry out basic one-variable and two-variable analyses.
  • Lab 3 shows you how to use RMarkdown to structure your work and create your report all in one document.
  • Lab 4 offers you an opportunity to practice and solidify your learning from the first three labs, and introduces more two-variable techniques.
  • Lab 5 shows you how to customize graphics outputs.
  • Lab 6 adds more graph customizations and shows you how to create new variables from existing ones.
